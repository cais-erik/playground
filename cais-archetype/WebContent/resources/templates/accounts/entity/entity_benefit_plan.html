Benefit Plan

The term "Benefit Plan Investors," as defined under Section 3(42) of ERISA and any regulations promulgated thereunder, includes:

  1. an "employee benefit plan" that is subject to the provisions of Title I of ERISA
  2. "plan" that is not subject to the provisions of Title I of ERISA, but that is subject to the prohibited transaction provisions of Section 4975 of the Code, such as individual retirement accounts and certain retirement plans for self-employed individuals; and
  3. a pooled investment fund whose assets are treated as "plan assets" under Section 3(42) of ERISA and any regulations promulgated thereunder because "employee benefit plans" or "plans" hold 25% or more of any class of equity interest in such pooled investment fund. For purposes of this Subscription Booklet, the term "Benefit Plan Investor" also includes an insurance company general account which is deemed under 29 C.F.R. § 2550.401c-1 to hold plan assets of the foregoing benefit plans and accounts as a result of the investment by such plans and accounts in such insurance company general account.
