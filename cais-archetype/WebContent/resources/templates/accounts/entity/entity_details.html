For purposes of FINRA Rule 4512(c), the term “institutional account” means the account of:

  1. A bank, savings and loan association, insurance company or registered investment company
  2. An investment advisor registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission under Section 203 of the Advisors Act or with a state securities commission (or any office performing like functions)
  3. Any other person (whether a natural person, corporation, partnership trust or otherwise) with total assets of at least $50 million.
