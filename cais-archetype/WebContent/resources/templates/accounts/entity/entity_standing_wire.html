The Financial Action Task Force (FATF) is an intergovernmental organization that develops policies and sets standards to combat money laundering and terrorism financing. The FATF monitors the progress of its members in implementing necessary measures, review money laundering and terrorist financing techniques and counter-measures, and promotes the adoption and implementation of appropriate measures globally. To see the list of FATF members, please visit: http://www.fatf-gafi.org/pages/aboutus/membersandobservers/
